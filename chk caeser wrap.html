Chicken Caeser Wrap

Description

This is a quick and easy lunch - chicken Caesar wraps are one of my favorites! If you don't already have croutons, whip up a batch in the oven in just a few quick minutes. Homemade croutons make this wrap extra tasty.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chopped romaine lettuce
  • ½ cup cooked, shredded chicken
  • ¼ cup croutons
  • 1 tablespoon Caesar's Salad Dressing
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large flour tortilla
    •

Steps:

  1. Combine lettuce, shredded chicken, croutons, dressing, and Parmesan cheese for the filling a a bowl. Toss gently to combine and everything is evenly coated with dressing. Adjust dressing amount to your personal preference - for me 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons is about right.
  2. Warm tortilla in the microwave for about 15 seconds to make it more pliable.
  3. Lay filling ingredients down the center, and roll your wrap up! Enjoy!
